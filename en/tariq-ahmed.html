Tariq Ahmed Rind

Architect, Urban Planning & Design Specialist

Tariq Ahmed Rind has a rich experience of working on multi-faceted urban and architectural design projects that include public/industrial buildings design, heritage preservation and conservation studies, design of public spaces. In addition, he has supervised planning and administration of major urban design projects and scope of engagement includes project identification, screening and appraisals, project planning and execution, zoning and densification studies, transportation planning and pedestrian mobility studies, physical planning and feasibility studies. Tariq recently served as the Urban Planning & Design Specialist in the Karachi Neighborhood Improvement Project (KNIP) – KNIP is being executed by the Government of Sindh and is funded by the World Bank. He acted as the design team lead for the design of the Cultural & Educational Zone in Saddar downtown area of Karachi city that included the design and implementation works on Peoples Square – a pioneering pedestrian district design project in Karachi. He also supervised the public space design and implementation works for 3 Km Roads/ urban space in Malir and Korangi areas. He also coordinated works of stakeholder consultations and design consultancies. Tariq has also acted as the Team Lead for Urban design and Landscape Improvement Project along Grand Trunk road from Jahangira to Peshawer Ring road, Khyber Pakhtunkhaw (KPK), executed by the Consultants Group. He has also worked with one of the leading consultancy firm in Pakistan – NESPAK in the capacity of Senior Urban Planner/Architect (Section Head – Planning), working on a number of multi-faceted projects.
