Dr. Syed Jamil Hasan Kazmi

Geographer, GIS Specialist (Meritorious Professor)

Dr. Syed Jamil Hasan Kazmi, is a Meritorious Professor and former Chairman (twice) of the Department of Geography and Ex-Director of ISPA, University of Karachi. He received his Doctoral Degree in Geography from University of Karachi (1997) and completed his Post-Doctoral term from University of Georgia, Athens under the Senior Fulbright Scholarship Program 1998-99. In addition, Prof. Kazmi has completed a Senior DAAD Research Fellow term at the Department of Geography, Humboldt University, Berlin, Germany in 2018-19. He has published extensively in journals of international repute in areas of application of Geo-informatics in environmental modeling and disease ecology. He has more than 120 publications to his credit (including four with Highest Impact Factor/Citations among any Pakistani Geography academic). Currently, he is the Editor of five International Journals of Geography, Ecology and Geo-Informatics and serves as the President of Pakistan Geographical Association (PGA). He was also the President of South Asian Chapter of Society of Urban Ecology (Austria) from 2017-2019. Kazmi is also a member of Association of American Geographers (AAG).

As a Professor he has supervised 30 PhDs and 16 MPhils in Geography, Ecology, Environmental Studies and Space Science. Dr. Kazmi has been associated as Faculty/PI and Co-PI/Research Associate with various international organizations such as University of British Columbia (Canada), University of Georgia (USA), Humboldt University, World Bank, UNDP, World Food Programme, FAO, ADB, EU, HEC, Ministry of Climate Change, SEPA, WWF, IUCN etc.

Furthermore, He has trained more than 6,000/ professionals nationally in GIS, Remote Sensing and Drone Technology. Dr. Kazmi is the recipient of prestigious awards such as “Special Achievement in GIS Award-2008” by ESRI, USA, “Best University Teacher Award-2007” by Higher Education Commission (HEC), Government of Pakistan and “Indus for All Ecology Award 2012” of WWF and many other honors, shields and momentous . He is the only Meritorious Professor of Geography in the Country since 2014.
