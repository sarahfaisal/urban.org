About Us

We provide an urban platform for action that aims to promote and execute solutions to urban livability challenges based on the Human Centered Design (HCD) approach. Where problems are addressed in a participatory, inclusive manner, and where a high level of empathy drives the process of understanding community challenges that are then addressed in a collaborative manner to optimize chances of sustainability of actions.

 Target is to improve key social, environmental and economic urban livability indicators and empower communities for informed collective action. A core focus is on building capacity (knowledge, skills, critical and strategic thinking) amongst the city youth so that they are catalyzed and capacitated to function as ‘game changers’ and ‘gate keepers’ of a more livable planet. A robust ‘student internship’ and ‘youth volunteer’ program is being initiated to leverage our youth as ‘change agents’. 

On the planning horizon is the establishment of an ‘IDEAS LAB’ – a physical space where youth work with the out of the box ideas and innovate to find solutions to urban livability challenges.  While in the beginning, Karachi city would mostly serve as the ‘lab’ for experimentation and practice, the long term vision is to spread this idea within the whole of urban Pakistan and globally, working with strategic partners across national, regional and international boundaries.  Modes of engagement would include advocacy and action research that we aim to achieve through social enterprise development, enhanced ‘networking’ by structuring collaborative partnerships with civil society, academic, private sector and government stakeholders to magnify the footprint of engagement and impact.
