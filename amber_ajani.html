Amber Ajani

Climate Change Consultant

Amber Ajani is a Fulbright alumna, a National Geographic Explorer and a former UN Climate Fellow with a master's degree in environmental science from the American University, USA. She has 8+ years of work experience spanning the United States, Brazil, Germany, and Pakistan. During her career, she has worked on areas of environmental education, integrated coastal management, environmental policy and communications, climate change, decarbonization, and sustainability strategy.
