Wajeeha Farooqi

Coordinator, Projects & Programs

Wajeeha Farooqi completed her Pharm-D degree from Jinnah Medical and Dental College, Karachi in the year 2012. Worked at Linz Pharmaceuticals as a Quality Assurance officer from where she also received training in Good Documentation Practices (GDP). She has conducted research on medicine usage from PCSIR laboratories. Later she completed a training session on conducting and documenting surveys. Wajeeha worked as a Project Coordinator with Sustainable Initiatives on projects funded by Karachi Youth Initiative (KYI) on health, community gardening and environmental art. She has also worked with Sustainable Initiatives on projects funded by Friedrich Naumann Foundation on climate change. During these projects, she provided training to stakeholders on Resource Efficient Urban Growth and Renewable Energy, and on Entrepreneurship and development of Social Action plans (SAP) specifically to the youth of underdeveloped areas. In collaboration with the NGO Health Initiatives (Agha Khan University Hospital), has assisted in organizing and supervising free medical camps in various schools of underdeveloped areas. Provided community based training in various underdeveloped areas on health awareness and youth volunteerism. She recently worked as a Marketing Coordinator in Sindh Trading Company
